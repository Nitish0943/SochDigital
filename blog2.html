Digital Chhattisgarh

Published on: January 10, 2024

As part of our vision to make Chhattisgarh digitally empowered, Digital Chhattisgarh focused on imparting practical skills in digital tools such as Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, and Canva. Conducted in Durg over a month, the program included workshops, hands-on demonstrations, and individual guidance. Each session highlighted how these tools could be applied in various fields, from education to small businesses. Aimed at individuals from all age groups, the program also addressed common digital challenges, such as online job applications and creating resumes. With over 200 participants completing the course, many reported newfound confidence in using these tools effectively. This initiative bridged the gap between traditional education and the practical digital skills required in today’s job market.
