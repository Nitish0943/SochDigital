Tech Sathi

Published on: January 10, 2024

Tech Sathi was designed to mentor students in creating their first tech projects, whether it be a website, a mobile app, or a basic hardware prototype. Conducted across multiple schools and colleges, the program paired participants with experienced mentors who guided them through brainstorming, planning, and execution. Over 30 students completed unique projects, including a website for local tourism and an IoT-based water level monitor. The highlight of the initiative was a community showcase where students presented their work to peers, parents, and local businesses. This initiative not only fostered technical skills but also built confidence and problem-solving abilities among participants.
