AI for All

Published on: January 10, 2024

This project aimed to make AI and machine learning accessible to beginners in Chhattisgarh. Conducted at a local library, it included a series of sessions introducing AI basics, ethical considerations, and hands-on practice with Python-based machine learning models. Participants worked on simple projects like spam detection and image recognition. The program was designed to break down complex concepts into easy-to-understand modules. Over 20 individuals from diverse backgrounds completed the course, and several expressed interest in pursuing advanced AI courses. This initiative opened doors for many to explore cutting-edge technologies that are shaping the future.
