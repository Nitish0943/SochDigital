Women in Tech

Published on: January 10, 2024

Women in Tech was an exclusive series of workshops designed to empower women in Bhilai with skills in UI/UX design. The program spanned four weeks, during which participants learned tools like Figma and Adobe XD through practical assignments. The workshops included real-world challenges such as redesigning interfaces for mobile apps and websites. Sessions were led by female industry experts who served as role models, inspiring participants to pursue tech careers. Over 25 women completed the program, with some already applying for internships in design firms. This initiative significantly boosted confidence and provided a platform for women to explore opportunities in the tech industry.
