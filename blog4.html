Future Coders Bootcamp

Published on: January 10, 2024

This two-week bootcamp aimed to equip rural youth with essential web development skills. Hosted in a community hall, the sessions covered HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, with a focus on building interactive and visually appealing websites. Participants were guided through real-world projects like creating personal portfolio websites or basic e-commerce platforms. The bootcamp provided laptops and internet access to ensure inclusivity. By the end, over 40 youth gained hands-on experience in web development, with many expressing interest in pursuing freelance opportunities or further education in technology.
